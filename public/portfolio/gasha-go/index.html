Gasha GO!

June 2022 - January 2024

Role Platform </> Client URL
Lead Developer iOS/Android TypeScript Georgia Public Broadcasting Link


Gasha GO is a collection of computer science games and animations designed to foster programmatic thinking and problem solving. It is built with TypeScript and Phaser 3, and uses Capacitor to create and manage native app versions of the game.

Gasha GO is keyboard accessible and available to play on the web on both desktop and mobile. It is currently available for free in the App Store and Google Play Store.
